## **How to Use “[Generate via AI](https://msg91.com/help/email/create-ai-powered-email-template-using-generate-via-ai)” in MSG91**

- Navigate to **Email \> Templates**

- Click **"Create Template"** and choose the **“Generate via AI”** option

- Enter a short description of your email as a prompt in the language you want
