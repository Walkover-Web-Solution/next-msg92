## **📌 What Is WhatsApp Coexistence?**

WhatsApp Coexistence allows your number to remain **active on your existing WhatsApp Business App** while simultaneously connecting to the **Cloud API** through platforms like MSG91.
#####
You get the **best of both worlds**—keep using your app and unlock the automation, campaign, and analytics power of MSG91.

## **⚠️ Important Notes**

-   **Messages are mirrored** between the app and MSG91’s Cloud API only for 1:1 chats (last 6 months).

-   **Group chats, calls, and business tools are not available** on Cloud API.

-   **Once you enable coexistence**, certain features (like disappearing messages, view-once media, and live location) will stop working on the app.
