## **What is WhatsApp OTP?**

WhatsApp OTP is a unique, single-use code sent directly to your customer’s WhatsApp. The user receives this code in real time, within a trusted app, ensuring fast and frictionless login, signup, or transaction approval—no extra steps, no switching screens.
