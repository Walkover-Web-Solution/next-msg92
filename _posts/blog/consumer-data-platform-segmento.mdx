## **1\. What Is a Consumer Data Platform?**

A consumer data platform (CDP) is a software system that collects data from every customer touchpoint—websites, apps, support chats, payment logs—and transforms it into one unified, reliable customer profile.

<span />
It’s not a CRM. It’s not a DMP. It’s your central intelligence hub.
<span />
While CRMs store individual transactions and conversations, and DMPs handle anonymous data for ads, CDPs like Segmento focus
on **first-party, real-time data** that lets you know what your customer did five seconds ago—and what they’re likely to
do next.
